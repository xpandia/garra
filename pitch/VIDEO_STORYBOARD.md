## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Recording
- Screen record the actual working demo at 1080p minimum, 60fps
- Use a clean browser with no bookmarks bar, no extensions visible
- Dark mode everything — terminal, browser, marketplace UI
- Record multiple takes; pick the smoothest transaction

### Voiceover
- Record in a quiet room, close to the mic
- Speak slowly and deliberately — this is a keynote, not an ad read
- Pause after "Until now." Let it breathe.
- Energy should build through Act 3 and peak at the payment confirmation

### Editing
- Hard cuts between text screens (no fancy transitions)
- Screen recordings can use subtle zoom-ins to highlight key UI elements
- Keep text on screen long enough to read at 1x speed
- Total silence for 0.5s before "Until now." and after payment confirms

### Music
- Suggestion: ambient electronic, minimal, builds slowly
- No lyrics. No heavy bass until the payment confirmation moment.
- Must be royalty-free or licensed

### Tools
- Screen recording: OBS or built-in macOS screen recorder
- Editing: DaVinci Resolve (free) or Final Cut Pro
- Voiceover: Voice Memos or Audacity
